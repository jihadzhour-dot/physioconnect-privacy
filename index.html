Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 10, 2026

PhysioConnect ("we", "our", or "us") operates the PhysioConnect mobile and desktop application (the "App"). This page explains what information we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect it.

1. Information We Collect

1a. Information you provide directly

  • Account information – name, email address, and password when you register.
  • Health and clinical data – physiotherapy session notes (SOAP notes), appointment records, diagnosis information, and treatment plans entered by doctors or patients.
  • Profile information – profile photo, phone number, date of birth, clinic name, and specialisation (for healthcare providers).

1b. Information collected automatically

  • Location – approximate or precise device location, collected only when you use the "Find a Doctor" or "Update My Location" features and only with your explicit permission. We do not track your location in the background.
  • Device token – a Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) token used solely to deliver push notifications about your appointments and clinical updates.
  • Usage data – basic error and crash information to help us improve the App. No analytics SDKs are embedded; this is limited to Supabase server-side logs.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the App and its features.
  • Match patients with their assigned physiotherapists and share relevant clinical notes between them.
  • Send appointment reminders and clinical update notifications.
  • Display nearby physiotherapists on the map (location data is stored only if you choose "Update My Location").
  • Generate PDF session reports and invoices at your request.
  • Improve the App based on aggregated, anonymised usage patterns.

3. How We Store and Protect Your Data

All data is stored on Supabase (PostgreSQL), hosted on secure cloud infrastructure with encryption at rest and in transit (TLS 1.2+). Access to health records is enforced by Row-Level Security (RLS) policies — each user can only read and write their own records.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal or health data with third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Health Data (Sensitive Personal Information)

PhysioConnect handles health-related information. We treat this data as sensitive personal information and apply the following protections:

  • Health records are accessible only to the patient and their assigned physiotherapist(s).
  • We do not disclose health data to any third party without your explicit written consent, except where required by law.
  • Clinical notes and SOAP records are never used for advertising or profiling purposes.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by using the "Delete My Account" option in the App or by contacting us at the email below. Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for use by healthcare professionals and adult patients. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your country).

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Object to or restrict certain processing activities.
  • Data portability (receive your data in a machine-readable format).

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at jihadzhour@gmail.com.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via an in-app notification or email. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page always reflects the most recent revision.
