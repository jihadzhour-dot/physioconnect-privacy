Privacy Policy

PhysioConnect  ·  Effective date: 19/6/2026  ·  Last updated: 19/6/2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Dr. Jihad Zour Physical Therapy Clinic ("we," "us," "the Clinic") collects, uses, stores, and protects personal and health information through the PhysioConnect mobile and desktop application ("the App"). We are committed to protecting the privacy of patients and practitioners in accordance with Lebanese Law No. 81/2018 on Electronic Transactions and Personal Data.

PhysioConnect is a clinic-management application that stores clinical patient records, including physiotherapy assessments and treatment notes. Because the App handles sensitive health data, this policy describes in detail what is collected and how it is safeguarded.

Where a practitioner uses the home-visit or location features, the App may collect approximate or precise device location. This is collected only when the feature is actively used and is optional.

A push-notification token (Firebase Cloud Messaging) used to deliver appointment and session reminders to your device.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information solely to operate the App and deliver clinical care, specifically to: maintain patient clinical records; schedule and manage appointments and sessions; produce billing and invoices; send appointment and session reminders; and enable role-based access for clinic staff (practitioners, polyclinic administrators, and managers). We do not sell personal or health data, and we do not use it for advertising.

4. How Your Data Is Stored and Processed (Sub-Processors)

We rely on the following third-party service providers to operate the App. Each processes data only as needed to provide its service:

Clinical data is stored in Supabase's managed PostgreSQL infrastructure. Access to health-data tables is restricted by row-level security so that each clinic and role can access only its own records.

5. Data Security

Data is encrypted in transit using TLS (HTTPS) for all communication between the App and our servers, and is encrypted at rest by our cloud database provider. Access controls limit data visibility according to user role. While we apply these safeguards, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

You may delete your account at any time from within the App. When an account is deleted, the associated account data is removed from our active systems immediately, including the records held under that account in our database.

Account deletion is available in-app for every account type — patient, practitioner, polyclinic administrator, and manager — under the account or settings section of each dashboard.

Residual copies may persist briefly in encrypted backups before being overwritten in the ordinary backup cycle. Where the Clinic is independently required by Lebanese professional or medical-record rules to retain certain clinical records outside the App, that obligation applies to the Clinic's own records and is separate from the deletion of your App account.

7. Your Rights

Under Lebanese Law No. 81/2018, you have the right to access the personal data we hold about you, request correction of inaccurate data, and request deletion of your data. You can exercise the access and deletion rights directly through the in-app account-deletion feature, or by contacting us at the email above for any other request.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is used by healthcare practitioners to manage patient care, which may include pediatric patients. In such cases, clinical records are entered and managed by the treating practitioner or the clinic, who are responsible for obtaining any consent required from a parent or legal guardian under applicable law. The App is not intended for independent use by children.
